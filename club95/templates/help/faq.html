An inactive event is an event that has already happened; these will only be viewable in My Events. A sold out event means all tickets are gone, preventing further purchases.

Open the My Tickets page from the top navigation. It lists your most recent orders first and lets you expand each order for the confirmation details.

Visit an event's detail page and click Purchase Tickets. Pick the tiers and quantities you need, review the order, and confirm the purchase. The tickets will then appear in My Tickets.

A ticket tier is a price level for the event. Different tiers can include perks such as drinks, reserved seating, or VIP access. Choose the tier that best fits the experience you want.
